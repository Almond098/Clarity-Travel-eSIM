Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our eSIM plans and services.

An eSIM (embedded SIM) is a digital SIM that allows you to activate a cellular plan without having to use a physical SIM card. It's built into your device and can be programmed remotely. To use our eSIM, simply purchase a plan, scan the QR code we provide, and follow the activation instructions on your device.

Most modern smartphones, tablets, and smartwatches support eSIM technology. This includes iPhone XS and newer, Google Pixel 3 and newer, Samsung Galaxy S20 and newer, and many other devices. You can check our compatibility page or your device manufacturer's specifications to confirm if your device supports eSIM.

Our travel eSIMs provide data connectivity only and do not include a phone number. However, most modern smartphones support Dual SIM functionality, allowing you to keep your regular SIM card with your phone number active while using our eSIM for data. This way, you can receive calls and texts on your regular number while using data from our eSIM.

After purchasing an eSIM plan, you'll receive a QR code via email. On your device, go to Settings > Cellular/Mobile Data > Add Cellular/Data Plan, then scan the QR code. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the activation. The process takes just a few minutes, and we provide detailed step-by-step guides for different device models.

Yes, you can purchase additional data or extend the validity period of your existing plan through your account dashboard. Simply log in, select your active plan, and choose the extension option. The additional data or time will be added to your existing plan without needing to activate a new eSIM.
